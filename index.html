What is this website about?

This is a platform for learning myself. I think maybe as we grow into adults, we feel like we wake up one morning and 'know' ourselves. After that fateful (and fictional) moment, we treat ourselves as static beings, acting like our interests, opinions, values will forever remain fixed and unchanged.

I believe we must continually update our knowledge about ourselves. A good way to do this may by laying the contents of ours minds before ourselves and analyzing what has built up over time.

This is what I'm doing with this website. It's just for fun! ☺
