What is this website about?

This is a platform for learning myself and sharing my interests.
It seems the moment we become adults, we feel as though we "know" ourselves. After such a moment, we perceive ourselves as static beings, pretending that our interests, opinions, and values will forever remain fixed and unchanged.

We are fluid, and it is best to be sensitive to this. The best way I have found to do this is by laying the contents of my mind before myself and analyzing what has built up or changed unnoticed over time.

This website is merely an exercise of this--it's all for fun! ☺
